1. Buy & download, import the following asset package:

https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/urban/polygon-city-low-poly-3d-art-by-synty-95214

2. Open the demo scene: 'PolygonCity/Scenes/Demo'.
3. Unpack the 'DotsCity/Packages/Integrations/PolygonCity/PolygonCity_Integration' package.
4. When the unpacking is finished, select the unpacked asset 'PolygonCity/DotsTemplate/PolygonCity_Integration' in the project view.
5. Click the 'Integrate' button in the Inspector.
6. Launch the scene.
7. Enjoy.
