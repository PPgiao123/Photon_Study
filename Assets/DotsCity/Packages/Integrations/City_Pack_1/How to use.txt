1. Buy & download, import the following asset package:

https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/urban/city-pack-1-202978

2. Open the demo scene: 'City_Pack_1/Scenes/City_Demonstration'.
3. Unpack the 'DotsCity/Packages/Integrations/City_Pack_1/City_Pack_1_Integration' package.
4. When the unpacking is finished, select the unpacked asset 'City_Pack_1/DotsTemplate/City_Pack_1_Integration' in the project view.
5. Click the 'Integrate' button in the Inspector.
6. Launch the scene.
7. Enjoy.
